// Knex
Knex libary is used to facility manipulation from db by migrates.
The migrates are interactions from your db like create table, drops, updates etc...
The first command you may understand is make, where your migrations are create in the project.
The second ones is command latest, where will exceute your previously create in your migrates files, to create your
tables if the parameters you choose.
The third one is the command rollback, this are called to rollback your migration to a previously version.
